Easy to Use

@redacted is designed to be easy to integrate and use in your current project: in three easy steps you will be all set up!

Focus on What Matters

Mark sensitive fields with @redacted and fear no more of mistakenly leak sensitive/PII infos in console, log, or as http/gRPC error responses!

Scala 3.x and 2.x Compatible!

@redacted supports a wide range of Scala 3.x versions (LTS & every highest version) as well as latest Scala 2.13.x and 2.12.x versions.
